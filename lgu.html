Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries

What documents do I need to prepare? +

You will need a valid government-issued ID (such as PhilSys ID, Passport, Driver's License), proof of residency in San Fernando, Pampanga (utility bill or barangay certificate), and program-specific documents that will be listed during the application process.

How does the AI verification system work? +

Our AI system uses advanced name matching algorithms and natural language processing to verify identities, detect similar names and potential duplicates across different programs. This helps ensure that assistance is distributed fairly and prevents multiple enrollments that violate program guidelines.

Can I apply for multiple assistance programs? +

Yes, you can apply for multiple programs, but our system will verify your eligibility based on each program's specific requirements and regulations regarding multiple benefits. Some programs may have restrictions on receiving simultaneous assistance.

What if I don't have internet access? +

We offer barangay-level assisted registration for residents with limited digital access. Visit your local barangay hall where authorized personnel can help you complete the application process using their facilities.

How long does the verification process take? +

The initial AI-powered verification typically takes 24-48 hours. After automated verification, LGU staff may conduct additional manual checks when necessary, which could take 3-5 business days. You will receive real-time updates throughout the process.
